About Us!


Are you an avid traveller, a thrill-seeker always up for an exciting adventure but always wasting a lot of time in choosing a good destination, working out how to reach, stay in a comfortable place and enjoy the best of the activities on offer! If your response to this is an affirmative then Universal Adventures is your best guide for an unforgettable experience. We are a one-point solution to a well crafted holiday - be it on the beach, in the snow or in a desert or even a tete a tete with the lions and leopards. Universal Adventures was created keeping our own experiences in mind, the problems we faced as a traveller during our holidays. Apart from just a good destination or a good place to stay there are many other concerns such as safety during night travel, good eating places, best activities you wouldn’t want to miss out on etc. Our customer care team will provide the best guidance possible that will give you an amazing holiday experience. If you are the independent kind and love to drive on your own we can even help you rent a suitable self-driving vehicle for your trip. We promise to help you make beautiful memories forever! If you would like to know what we are doing right now, follow us at Facebook & Instagram.
